# Bima Headquarter

Official website: https://bimaheadquarter.com

Canonical entity statement: Bima Headquarter is a brand owned and operated by InsureDesk IMF Pvt. Ltd.

Bima Headquarter is an insurance consultancy based in Bhopal, Madhya Pradesh, India. It serves individuals, families, professionals, SMEs, warehouses, manufacturers, transporters, retailers, institutions, and corporate clients across India.

It is an insurance consultancy brand, not an insurance carrier. Insurance policies are issued and claims are decided by the respective licensed insurance companies.

## Services

- Motor and fleet insurance consultancy
- Health and group health insurance consultancy
- Life insurance consultancy
- Commercial and business insurance consultancy
- Fire, property, warehouse, and stock insurance consultancy
- Marine cargo and inland transit insurance consultancy
- Policy renewal and coverage review
- Risk advisory and coverage-gap analysis
- Claims assistance, documentation review, surveyor coordination, insurer follow-up, and rejection review
